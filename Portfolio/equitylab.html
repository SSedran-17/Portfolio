“Captain Weaver” is an aid in the beginning part of basket weaving. In the handheld mode, the reed or cordage is woven around the needle on top of our prototype to create the base. As the user is weaving they can rotate the needle and therefore the base of the basket by using their thumb to push the “pirate ship wheel”. This allows the user to cut down on the harmful pinching motion needed for basket weaving. My team and I designed Captain Weaver, and I CADed it using Autodesk Fusion 360.
