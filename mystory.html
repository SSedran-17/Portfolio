My Story
My name is Samantha Sedran, I am a second year Engineering Science Robotics Major at the University of Toronto. When I was ten, I took a one-week summer robotics camp and I fell in love with robots. That week changed my life. Soon after, my friend and I started our own middle school VEX IQ robotics team. We competed for three years and won over 30 awards at regional, national, and internatinal levels. Through our competitions, friend and I noticed the large gender gap in robotics. We started a GoFundMe to raise money to send girls to the robotics camp we attended: More Information.
In high school, I joined my school's FIRST robotics team FRC 7558 ALT-F4. I learned SOLIDWORKS and Autodesk Fusion 360 on this team. As of 2025 I have amassed 7 years of CAD experience. From September 2022 to June 2023 I ran a weekly after school club to teach girls how to build VEX IQ robotics: More Information. In September 2023, I started at the University of Toronto in Engineering Science and they awarded me the Schulich Leader Scholarship. I continue to pursue my passion of robotics through my work on the University of Toronto Robotics Association Combat Team and by majoring in robotics next year.
